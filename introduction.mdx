# Welcome to Karma POS

Karma POS is a powerful, feature-rich Point of Sale (POS) system designed for retail businesses that need a comprehensive solution for sales, inventory management, and customer relationships. Built with cutting-edge technologies including Angular 19, PrimeNG, and TailwindCSS, Karma POS delivers a modern, responsive interface with enterprise-grade functionality.

## What is Karma POS?

Karma POS is a full-featured retail management platform that streamlines your business operations from the ground up. It provides everything you need to run a successful retail operation, from processing sales and managing inventory to tracking customer relationships and generating business insights.

The system is designed to handle the complexities of modern retail, including:

- Multi-invoice management with concurrent customer transactions
- Real-time stock reservations and inventory tracking
- Flexible payment methods including cash, card, and financing options
- Customer management with detailed transaction history
- Role-based permissions for secure multi-user access
- Comprehensive reporting and analytics

## Key Features

### Core POS Functionality

- **Multi-Invoice Management**: Handle multiple customers simultaneously with separate shopping carts
- **Barcode Scanning**: Quick product lookup via barcode or product code
- **Real-Time Stock Management**: Automatic stock reservations prevent overselling
- **Flexible Payments**: Support for cash, card, financing, and mixed payment methods
- **Invoice Generation**: Professional PDF invoices with company branding

### Inventory & Purchasing

- **Product Management**: Comprehensive product catalog with categories, pricing, and stock levels
- **Low Stock Alerts**: Automatic notifications when inventory runs low
- **Purchase Orders**: Streamlined purchasing workflow with supplier management
- **Multi-Warehouse Support**: Track inventory across multiple locations

### Customer Management

- **Customer Database**: Maintain detailed customer records with contact information
- **Transaction History**: Complete purchase history for each customer
- **Financing Options**: Offer payment plans with automated payment tracking
- **WhatsApp Integration**: Multi-agent WhatsApp chat for customer support

### Business Intelligence

- **Reports & KPIs**: Comprehensive analytics and business metrics
- **Cash Register Management**: Track cash flow and accounting
- **Sales Analytics**: Detailed sales reports by product, category, and time period
- **Promotion Management**: Create and track promotional campaigns

### Security & Access Control

- **Role-Based Permissions**: Granular control over module access and actions
- **User Management**: Multi-user support with individual permissions
- **Secure Authentication**: Protected login with session management
- **Audit Trail**: Track user actions and changes

## Technology Stack

Karma POS is built on a modern technology stack that ensures performance, maintainability, and scalability:

- **Frontend Framework**: Angular 19 with standalone components
- **UI Library**: PrimeNG 19 with Aura theme
- **Styling**: TailwindCSS with PrimeUI integration
- **State Management**: RxJS for reactive data flow
- **Real-Time Updates**: SignalR for live notifications
- **PDF Generation**: jsPDF and pdfmake for invoice generation
- **TypeScript**: Full type safety with strict mode enabled

## System Architecture

The application follows a modular architecture with clear separation of concerns:

```
src/
├── app/
│   ├── components/
│   │   ├── pages/          # Feature modules
│   │   │   ├── pos-folder/ # Point of Sale
│   │   │   ├── inventory/  # Inventory management
│   │   │   ├── purchases/  # Purchase orders
│   │   │   ├── reports/    # Analytics
│   │   │   └── ...         # Other modules
│   │   └── services/       # Shared services
│   ├── layout/             # Application shell
│   └── auth/               # Authentication
└── assets/                 # Static resources
```
